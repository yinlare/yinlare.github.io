Biograhpy

Liying (Ying) Li got her bachelor's degree in Commerce and Finance from the University of Toronto and is a former banking professional. As a long time art enthusiast, she decided to make a career transition after a trip to Nepal in 2015.

Ying has studied Modern and Contemporary Art and Art Business in New York since 2016. She has learned that the art journey never stops: she’s exploring Old Master art and curatorial practices at varied institutions. She hopes to help emerging artists showcase their art in the future.

Ying is also a wine lover and a cat fancier. Currently based in New York City, she loves traveling and exploring new cultures.
