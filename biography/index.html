Biograhpy

Ying is a former banking professional, a cat fancier and an art lover. She was born and raised in China, and spent a few years in the lovely country Canada.

During a trip in Nepal in 2015, she decided to make a career change. The next year, she started studying art history and art business in New York with a focus on Modern and Contemporary Art.

She loves Manet’s bold contours, Laurencin’s pastel subtlety, Matisse’s color improvisation, Hopper’s solitude, de Kooning’s wild brushstrokes, and the list goes on. The art journey is going on - currently she is exploring the art auction arena, Renaissance, and art finance.
